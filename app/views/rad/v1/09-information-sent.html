Thank you for reporting John Smith’s death to DWP. Please take a note of your reference number in case you need to contact us. Using this service does not form any part of a claim to benefits.

What happens next

DWP have been informed of the death to adjust pensions and other benefits. If they need more information - including bank details - they will contact you, or the spouse, using the contact details you supplied.

You may also be eligible for bereavement support and help with funeral costs, DWP will contact you to confirm the info you have provided and to find out if you’re eligible for financial help. More information about financial support can be found below.

Continue to use Report a Death

This service can also inform other departments and organisations ahead of your registration appointment. This will save you time and prevent you having to inform each one separately. The information you provide will not be submitted until after the registration is complete.
