Before you start

This service helps you to report the death of Mr John Smith to government organisations, local councils, pension holders (public and private sector), and other entities before you have attended your appointment at the Milton Keynes register office.

Report a Death step by step overview

  1. Step 1:

    Report the death to DWP to stop pension payments and understand the financial impact on you

  2. Step 2:

    Prepare for your appointment by entering information about John Smith to share with other Government departments after the appointment. You can do this at any time before the appointment.

  3. Step 3:

    Attend your registration appointment

  4. Step 4:

    After the death is officially registered by the Registrar, the other Government departments will be told about the death
