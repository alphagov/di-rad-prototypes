Thank you for booking your appointment to register the death of Mr John Smith on;

17th of August at 13:00 at Milton Keynes City Council Register Office.

Ahead of the appointment there are some things you can do online, using the new Report a Death service, which will save you time in your appointment and help to avoid delays with government admin.

The first part of this service is to tell Department of Work and Pensions (DWP) that John Smith has died, which saves you having to call them to adjust any pension payments that may have been sent after he died.

The service will also direct you to financial assistance that you may be entitled to from DWP.
