Start an employment check

Use this service to confirm your eligibility to work in the UK and share it with your employer. This service is free to use. It’s available for British citizens and people with a valid UK working visa.

Using this service will not affect your right to work in the UK.

You can use at least one of the following to prove your eligibility to work:

  • A valid UK passport
  • A photo identification document issued in another country
  • An active UK Visa (Skilled Worker Visa, Business Visitor Visa, etc)
  • A Basic DBS check
  • National Insurance Number
