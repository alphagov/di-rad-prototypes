What happens next

The organisations you have told about the death will update their records. They will not need to see the death certificate.

Check if you can get financial help

You could be eligible for help with funeral costs if you get certain benefits.
If your husband, wife or civil partner has died, and you're under State Pension age, you could be entitled to Bereavement Support Payment.
You can call the DWP Bereavement Service free of charge and they can check if you're eligible and make an application.

Emotional help and support

There are organisations who can help if you're dealing with a bereavement, or supporting someone who is.

Cruse Bereavement Care

Cruse offer emotional support to anyone affected by bereavement:

Telephone: freephone 0808 808 1677

Website: www.cruse.org.uk

Scotland has a dedicated Cruse helpline and website:

Telephone: freephone 0808 802 6161

Website: www.crusescotland.org.uk

All calls are free of charge

The Silver Line

The Silver Line offers a free confidential helpline for older people which is open 24 hours a day.

Telephone: 0800 470 8090

Website: www.thesilverline.org.uk
